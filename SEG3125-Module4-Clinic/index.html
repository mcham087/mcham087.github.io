About us

Bayview Animal Hospital is a small animal hospital located in the Greater Toronto Area. Our team of highly trained veterinarians and staff are proud to offer a wide variety of services.

We are dedicated to providing the best care to you and your pet.

Our Services

Annual wellness exams allow our veterinarians to identify potential issues in time to prevent them from becoming major illnesses. If your pet requires any other procedures, our veterinarians will consult you before proceeding.

Cost: $100

Taking care of your pet's teeth can prevent many major illnesses. But even with regular brushing, plaque and tartar can accumulate on the gums. We are equipped with a top class dental operation room and will ensure your pet comes out with bright pearly whites.

Cost: $200

Vaccinations boost your pet's immune system and are essential to keeping them safe. Our veterinarians will help you plan the appropriate course of vaccinations over its lifetime.

Cost: $70

Spays and neuters can prevent pregnancies and provide health benefits. In females, spaying decreases the chance of uterine infections and mammary cancer. In males, neutering reduces the chance of prostate and testicular cancer. We will ensure that your pet has a painless and comfortable experience as possible.

Cost: $600

We perform oral, soft-tissue and mass removal surgeries. We equipped with the latest and most advanced technology in our operating room. We will ensure that your pet is as comfortable as possible during the procedure and that they receive the best aftercare.

Cost: $1000
